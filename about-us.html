About Us

Lombardo Construction

Daniel grew up in Boston Massachusetts and moved to Florida in the late ’90s. He spent his life in the construction industry and developed a passion for home building. For over 30 years, Daniel Lombardo has been earning the trust of clients here in Pinellas County by providing them with the highest level of quality and attention they are expecting. While many other contractors may be challenging to coordinate with, Daniel understands that construction on your home comes with many questions on your part. He believes transparency and superior client communication is of the utmost importance. Whether it's a remodel, addition or new home construction, Daniel will be by your side to answer questions and walk you through each stage of your home project. You can rest assured that you are in good hands. .
